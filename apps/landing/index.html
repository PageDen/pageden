Features

Built for humans and agents alike

Everything your team needs to write, organize, and expose knowledge safely to AI.

🤖
MCP-ready for AI agents
Connect Claude, Codex, or any MCP-compatible agent directly to your knowledge base. Permissioned, auditable, safe.
🔗
Obsidian sync
Write in Obsidian, sync to Pageden. Your local vault becomes your team's shared source of truth — no migration needed.
✏️
Markdown-native editor
A fast editor with slash commands, rich embeds, tables, and full Markdown support. What you write is what agents read.
🔒
Permissions & access
Granular read/write permissions by page, collection, or team. Control exactly what humans — and agents — can see.
📅
Version history
Every change is saved and diffable. Know exactly what your agents are reading, and roll back any time.
🌐
Open source
100% open source. Self-host for complete data ownership, or use our managed cloud. Your knowledge stays yours.
